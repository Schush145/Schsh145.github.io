MONKEY TRAIL

Take a walk or ride through this challenging obstacle course.

The Monkey Trail is a series of trails just outside of Lost Forest that caters to both mountain bikers and traditional hikers, and it's just perfect this time of year. Let's check it out:

  • There are over 10 miles of trails to explore, and they’re ideal for mountain biking. The trails are mainly single track, with plenty of rolling hills and switchbacks to keep your blood pumping..
  • There’s also a 1.5-km, 3-km, 5-km, 10-km and 15-km's hiking paths.

The trails are well-marked, but it doesn’t hurt to have an AllTrails Plus account before you head out.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Monkey Trails is found just outside the Lost Forest on the west entrance
